SKILL.md
/growth-sprint

Weekly growth sprint ritual. Pick one product and push it.

Philosophy

Marketing as a loop, not a vibe: Signal → Offer → Distribution → Measurement → Iterate

The Sprint Checklist

Include these sections with checkboxes:

Content (3 posts, same idea, different wrapper)
 Twitter/X: hot take + demo link
 Reddit: problem-first + "here's what I built"
 Indie Hackers / HN: builder log + numbers/learning
Distribution (1 submission)
 Product Hunt (when ready)
 Hacker News (Show HN)
 Relevant subreddit
 Niche directory/list
Outreach (10 DMs)
 Not "buy my thing"
 "I built X for Y — mind telling me if this solves your problem? 2 questions."
Measure
 Visits: ___
 Signups: ___
 Activations: ___
 Paid: ___
Process
Ask which product to focus on this week
Check for existing brand-profile.yaml (if not exists, suggest /brand-builder)
Walk through each checklist section interactively
For content: generate drafts using brand profile if available
For distribution: prepare templates (NOT auto-post to Reddit/HN - ban risk)
For outreach: help identify 10 DM targets
Log results to growth-sprint-log.md (append to existing or create)
Summarize and set next week's focus
