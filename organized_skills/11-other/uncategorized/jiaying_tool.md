Jiaying Tool (剪映工具)
Overview

Jiaying (CapCut) is Xiaohongshu's preferred video editing tool - a free, feature-rich app that enables creators to produce professional-quality short videos with effects, transitions, subtitles, and music optimization for mobile viewing.

When to Use

Use when:

Creating short videos for Xiaohongshu (under 5 minutes)
Adding effects and transitions to video clips
Adding subtitles or text overlays
Adding background music or sound effects
Adjusting video speed or applying filters
Creating before-and-after comparison videos

Do NOT use when:

Need advanced color grading (use DaVinci Resolve)
Editing long-form video over 10 minutes (use Premiere/Final Cut)
Need complex 3D effects (use After Effects)
Core Pattern

Before (raw, unedited footage):

❌ Single long take, boring
❌ No subtitles, hard to understand
❌ Poor audio, inconsistent volume
❌ No effects or transitions


After (professionally edited):

✅ 15-60 seconds, tightly edited
✅ Clear subtitles and text overlays
✅ Balanced audio with music
✅ Smooth transitions and engaging effects

Implementation
Step 1: Import and Organize Footage

Open Jiaying:

Download Jiaying app (App Store or Google Play)
Tap "Start Creating" (开始创作)
Select video clips from camera roll
Tap "Add" (添加) to import

Organize clips:

Drag to reorder clips
Preview full sequence
Trim unwanted parts (see Step 2)
Step 2: Trim and Cut Clips

Basic editing:

Trim start/end: Tap clip, drag yellow handles
Split clip: Tap clip → tap "Split" (分割) at playhead position
Delete section: Tap unwanted section → tap "Delete" (删除)

Pro tip: Trim to 15-60 seconds for optimal Xiaohongshu performance (attention spans are short).

Step 3: Add Text and Subtitles

Add title:

Tap "Text" (文字) button
Choose text style (recommended: bold, white with black outline)
Type title text
Adjust size and position (center top or bottom)
Set duration (when text appears/disappears)

Add subtitles:

Tap "Text" → "Auto Captions" (自动识别字幕)
Let AI transcribe audio (free feature)
Edit mistakes if needed
Choose subtitle style (white text, black outline recommended)
Position at bottom (don't cover important content)

Text overlays:

Use sparingly (3-5 text overlays max per video)
Key info only (product names, prices, key tips)
Keep on screen 2-3 seconds minimum (readable)
Step 4: Add Music and Audio

Add background music:

Tap "Audio" (音频) → "Music" (音乐)
Browse or search for music
Tap "+" to add to project
Adjust volume (10-20% of voice volume)
Trim music to match video length

Volume balancing:

Tap "Volume" (音量) icon
Set original audio to 100%
Set music to 10-20% (background, not distraction)
Preview to ensure voice is clear

Sound effects (optional):

Tap "Audio" → "Effects" (音效)
Add transitions sounds (whoosh, pop, etc.)
Use sparingly (1-2 per video)
Step 5: Apply Effects and Filters

Color correction:

Tap "Filters" (滤镜)
Choose filter (recommended: "Natural" or "Bright")
Adjust intensity (50-70% for subtle enhancement)

Effects (optional):

Tap "Effects" (特效)
Browse categories (beauty, blur, artistic, etc.)
Tap effect to preview
Adjust intensity if available

Pro tip: Less is more. Subtle effects look more professional than heavy filters.

Step 6: Add Transitions

Between clips:

Tap transition icon between two clips
Choose transition type:
"Fade" (淡入淡出) - subtle, professional
"Dissolve" (叠化) - smooth, elegant
"Wipe" (擦除) - dynamic, engaging
Adjust duration (0.3-0.5 seconds recommended)

Pro tip: Use consistent transitions throughout video (don't mix many types).

Step 7: Adjust Speed (Optional)

Slow motion:

Tap clip → tap "Speed" (变速)
Choose speed below 1x (0.5x = half speed, 0.25x = quarter speed)
Use for: dramatic moments, detailed demonstrations

Speed up:

Tap clip → tap "Speed"
Choose speed above 1x (2x = double speed)
Use for: boring parts, processes, time-lapses

Pro tip: Use speed changes sparingly. Most content should be normal speed (1x).

Step 8: Export Video

Final preview:

Watch full video from start to finish
Check for: smooth cuts, clear audio, readable text, appropriate length
Make adjustments if needed

Export settings:

Tap "Export" (导出) button
Resolution: 1080p (recommended) or 4K if footage supports
Frame rate: 30fps (standard) or 60fps (smooth motion)
Codec: H.264 (standard, compatible)
Tap "Export" to save

Upload to Xiaohongshu:

Open Xiaohongshu app
Tap "+" → select video from camera roll
Add title, description, hashtags
Post!
Common Mistakes
Mistake	Why Happens	Fix
Video too long	Want to show everything	Cut to 15-60 seconds
Too many effects	Trying to be creative	Use 1-2 effects max
Music too loud	Sounds better in editing	Reduce to 10-20% volume
Subtitles too small	Aesthetic choice	Use large, readable text
Inconsistent transitions	Didn't plan ahead	Choose 1 style, use throughout
