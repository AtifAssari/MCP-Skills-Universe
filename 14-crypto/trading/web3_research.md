Research Guidelines
Analysis Framework

When researching Web3 topics, follow this structured approach:

Market Overview: Current price, market cap, trading volume, market sentiment
Technology Analysis: Protocol architecture, consensus mechanism, scalability solutions
Tokenomics Review: Token supply, distribution, utility, inflation/deflation mechanics
Ecosystem Assessment: DApps, partnerships, developer activity, community health
Risk Analysis: Security concerns, regulatory risks, competition, centralization risks
Investment Thesis: Bull case, bear case, key catalysts, price targets
Analysis Types
Fundamental Analysis

Focus on:

Team background and track record
Technology innovation and roadmap
Token utility and value accrual

Revenue models and treasury health
Technical Analysis

Focus on:

Price action and chart patterns
Support and resistance levels
Volume trends and momentum indicators
On-chain metrics (TVL, active addresses, transaction volume)
Sentiment Analysis

Focus on:

Social media trends and mentions
Developer activity (GitHub commits, releases)
Whale movements and accumulation patterns
News and media coverage

Research Best Practices
Always verify information from multiple sources
Distinguish between facts and speculation - crypto markets are full of rumors
Consider the source bias - many crypto outlets have financial incentives
Look for on-chain data when possible for objective metrics
Be cautious with price predictions - acknowledge uncertainty
Highlight regulatory considerations relevant to the jurisdiction
Disclose if information may be outdated - crypto moves fast
