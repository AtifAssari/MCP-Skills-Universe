Competitor Analysis

When asked to analyze competitors:

Identify the top 3-5 competitors in the target segment
For each competitor, assess:
Product positioning and key differentiators
Pricing model and tiers
Target audience and market share estimates
Strengths and weaknesses
Create a comparison matrix
Identify gaps and opportunities for differentiation
