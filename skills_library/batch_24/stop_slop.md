Stop Slop

Eliminate predictable AI writing patterns from prose. Apply these rules to any writing task.

Core Principles
Eliminate filler - Remove predictable opening phrases and emphasis words
Avoid formulaic patterns - Sidestep binary contrasts and dramatic structures
Vary sentence rhythm - Mix lengths and paragraph endings
Write directly - Trust readers without excessive explanation
Remove quotable moments - Rewrite anything that reads like a pull-quote
Quick Audit Before Finalizing
Check for repetitive sentence lengths
Look for monotonous paragraph endings
Remove unnecessary em-dashes
Cut over-explained metaphors
Assessment Framework

Rate prose 1-10 on each dimension:

Directness - Does it get to the point?
Rhythm - Does sentence length vary naturally?
Trust - Does it respect reader intelligence?
Authenticity - Does it sound like a human wrote it?
Density - Is every word earning its place?

Below 35/50: revise.
